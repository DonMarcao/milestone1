ARMORY - ROUTES

Urban Combat Map

The Armory is a medium-sized urban environment featuring a central military installation surrounded by residential and commercial buildings. This map excels in providing multiple approach angles and vertical gameplay opportunities. Success requires understanding high-traffic areas, optimal rotation paths, and extraction point positioning. The central armory building contains high-value loot but attracts heavy enemy presence.

Key Features

Extraction Points

  • South Gate (most common, moderate risk)
  • Rooftop Helipad (requires key, high risk)
  • Underground Tunnel (hidden, low risk)
  • East Checkpoint (quick access, high visibility)

High-Value Loot Spots

  • Armory Vault - weapons & attachments
  • Command Center - intelligence items
  • Officer Quarters - cash & valuables
  • Supply Depot - medical & tactical gear

Safe Routes

  • Perimeter Path - avoid central combat
  • Rooftop Network - elevated movement
  • Building Interiors - cover from snipers
  • Drainage System - underground passage

FARM - ROUTES

Open Terrain Map

Farm is a sprawling rural environment featuring open fields, scattered buildings, and forested areas. This map heavily favors long-range combat and tactical positioning. Movement across open areas requires careful timing and awareness of sniper positions. The map's size allows for multiple simultaneous engagements across different zones. Understanding terrain elevation and natural cover is essential for survival.

Key Features

Extraction Points

  • Main Road (vehicle required, exposed)
  • Forest Edge (on foot, concealed)
  • Barn Loft (conditional, limited time)
  • River Crossing (water route, slow)

High-Value Loot Spots

  • Main Farmhouse - residential loot
  • Equipment Shed - tools & attachments
  • Silo Storage - hidden caches
  • Worker Cabins - medical supplies

Safe Routes

  • Tree Line Movement - forest cover
  • Ditch Network - low profile travel
  • Building-to-Building - minimize exposure
  • Night Movement - reduced visibility

FORT NARITH - ROUTES

Military Installation Map

Fort Narith is a heavily fortified military complex featuring multi-level structures, underground bunkers, and defensive positions. This map is characterized by intense close to mid-range combat with limited sightlines. The fort's design creates natural chokepoints and contested zones. High-tier loot attracts aggressive players, making every raid a high-stakes operation requiring excellent tactics and loadout preparation.

Key Features

Extraction Points

  • Main Gate (heavily defended)
  • Bunker Exit (underground route)
  • Watchtower Rope (requires item)
  • Breach Point (explosive needed)

High-Value Loot Spots

  • Command Bunker - rare equipment
  • Arsenal - tier 6-7 weapons
  • Communications Room - intelligence
  • Generator Room - tech loot

Danger Zones

  • Central Courtyard - deadly crossfire
  • Main Building - CQB hell
  • Perimeter Walls - sniper positions
  • Underground - maze-like corridors

NORTHRIDGE - ROUTES

Urban Map

Northridge is an urban warfare environment featuring residential blocks, commercial centers, and underground metro systems. The map's complexity comes from its verticality and interconnected buildings. Rooftop access, window positions, and interior navigation create a three-dimensional battlefield where threats can come from any angle. Knowledge of optimal routes is essential for survival and successful extraction.

Key Features

Extraction Points

  • Metro Station (underground route)
  • Rooftop Evacuation (vertical)
  • Street Checkpoint (patrolled)
  • Parking Garage (vehicle)

High-Value Loot Spots

  • Bank Vault - cash & valuables
  • Police Station - tactical gear
  • Hospital - medical supplies
  • Electronics Store - tech loot

Safe Routes

  • Alleyway Network - covered movement
  • Sewer System - underground travel
  • Rooftop Pathway - elevated route
  • Building Interiors - avoid streets

TV STATION - ROUTES

Close-Quarters/Interior Map

The TV Station is a highly compact and dangerous interior map defined by tight corridors, multi-level studios, and numerous sightlines. Combat is almost exclusively close-quarters, demanding excellent reaction time and sound awareness. The map's design heavily favors ambush tactics and careful room clearing. Key objectives are often heavily defended by high-tier operatives, making every push a high-stakes gamble.

Key Features

Extraction Points

  • Loading Dock (highly contested, vehicle)
  • Studio Ventilation Shaft (one-time use, limited access)
  • Heliport Rooftop (conditional, flares needed)
  • Main Entrance (risky, heavily exposed)

High-Value Loot Spots

  • Server Room - valuable electronics & data chips
  • Director's Office - key cards & cash safes
  • News Anchor Desk - small, quick-grab valuables
  • Security Checkpoint - high-tier armor/weapons caches

Safe Routes

  • Maintenance Tunnels - avoid central areas, slow
  • Back Stairwells - quieter movement between floors
  • Vents/Ducts - limited access, stealth movement
  • Fire Escape (Exterior) - bypass interior choke points
