Extraction Maps - Guides and Routes

Farm Map

Farm is a small, high-intensity map with multiple engagement zones, making it popular for both quick raids and intense PvP. The map layout favors close-to-medium range weapons.

Extraction Points

  • North Exit (Always Open)
  • Southern Road (Requires Key)
  • Factory Exit (Timed / Conditional)
  • Armored Vehicle (Flares Required)

Danger Zones

The **Warehouse Complex** and **Motel** are high-value loot zones, expecting heavy opposition. Approach with caution and suppression gear.

Valley Map

Valley is known for its diverse terrain, including rural areas and a dense forest, encouraging long-range engagement and flanking maneuvers. Visibility can be low in the forest, favoring players with thermal gear.

Extraction Points

  • Roadside (Always Open)
  • Mountain Pass (Requires Gear)
  • River Dock (Conditional)
  • Air Drop (Random Event)

Danger Zones

The **North Bridge** and the **Valley Dam** are critical bottlenecks. The Dam offers high-tier loot but is extremely exposed to snipers.

Northridge Map

Northridge is the largest map, offering vast, open spaces perfect for long-range rifles and squad cooperation. Key points are far apart, making vehicles or careful planning essential for loot runs and extractions.

Extraction Points

  • Bus Station (Always Open)
  • Observatory (Timed / Conditional)
  • Main Entrance (Conditional)
  • Heliport (Requires Cooperation)

Danger Zones

The **Observatory** and the **Dam Bridge** are the main points of contention. Be aware of players holding long angles from the surrounding mountains.
