Assault Rifle

The HK416-Carbine is a top-tier assault rifle known for its reliability and versatility. With excellent accuracy and manageable recoil, it excels in both mid and close-range combat situations. This weapon is a favorite among experienced operators for its balanced performance across all engagement distances.

TIER 7

SJ16 .338 Sniper Rifle

Bolt-Action Sniper Rifle

The SJ16 is the most powerful and expensive bolt-action sniper rifle in Arena Breakout Infinite, firing the massive .338 Lapua Magnum round. This weapon is specialized for one thing: guaranteed destruction of any opponent at range, regardless of their armor tier.

Recommended Attachments

MUZZLE - .338 Muzzle Brake

Stability & Recoil Reduction. The brake helps minimize the massive muzzle rise for faster target re-acquisition. A suppressor is often used for high-cost stealth.

OPTIC - 6x-20x Variable Scope

Extreme Range Precision. This magnification range is essential for taking full advantage of the .338 round's low drop and extreme range on large maps like Northridge.

GRIP/MOUNT - Bipod

Maximized Stability. A Bipod is crucial for minimizing scope sway and making the gun a stable laser when prone or resting on an object, which is how the SJ16 is primarily used.

STOCK - SJ16 Tactical Stock

Max Recoil Dampening. The best dedicated stock to reduce the bolt-action kick, improving the ability to spot the enemy after the shot is fired.

BARREL - Longest Available Barrel

Maximized Muzzle Velocity. Guarantees the round hits with maximum speed and energy, ensuring the one-shot kill on high-tier armor.
