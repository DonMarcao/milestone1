SEASON 5 ANNOUNCED: 'FROZEN FRONTIER'

Posted: November 15, 2025 | Category: Major Update

The developers have just released a major roadmap detailing Season 5: Frozen Frontier. This update is expected to introduce a new snow-covered map variant and focus heavily on survival mechanics and temperature regulation. Players must now manage a new Hypothermia gauge when exposed to outdoor elements for extended periods.

Key new features include:

  • New 'Frozen' version of the Northridge Map.
  • Introduction of thermal clothing and heat packs.
  • Reworked movement penalties based on gear weight.
  • A new high-tier sniper rifle (M200 Intervention).
