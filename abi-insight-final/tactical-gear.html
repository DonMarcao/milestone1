Tactical Gear - Armor, Helmets, and Utilities

Class 6 Armor: Aegis Heavy Vest

The Aegis Heavy Vest represents the best ballistic protection available, offering superior defense against high-caliber rounds. This vest is essential for high-risk engagements in maps like Armory and TV Station.

Specifications

STATISTIC VALUE
Protection Level 6 (MAX)
Durability 300 Points
Effective vs. 7.62x51mm, 5.56x45mm (AP)
Movement Penalty -20% Speed, -15% Ergonomics

Class 4 Armor: Lightweight Assault Vest

The Lightweight Assault Vest is the best all-around armor for budget-conscious players. It offers solid protection against common rounds while maintaining decent mobility for quick flanking maneuvers and repositioning.

Specifications

STATISTIC VALUE
Protection Level 4 (Balanced)
Durability 200 Points
Effective vs. 9x19mm (AP), 5.56x45mm (Standard)
Movement Penalty -10% Speed, -5% Ergonomics

Class 5 Helmet: Spectre Full-Coverage

The Spectre Helmet offers best-in-class protection for the head, covering both the top and face with Level 5 ballistic plates. It is a critical piece of gear for surviving headshots from assault rifle rounds.

Specifications

STATISTIC VALUE
Protection Level 5 (High)
Durability 120 Points
Coverage Head & Face
Hearing Penalty Moderate
