Class 6 Armor: Aegis Heavy Vest Tier S

The Aegis Heavy Vest represents the best ballistic protection available, offering superior defense against high-caliber rounds. This vest is essential for high-risk engagements in maps like Armory and TV Station.

Class 4 Armor: Lightweight Assault Vest Tier B

Class 5 Helmet: Spectre Full-Coverage Tier A+

The Spectre Helmet offers best-in-class protection for the head, covering both the top and face with Level 5 ballistic plates. It is a critical piece of gear for surviving headshots from assault rifle rounds.

STATISTIC VALUE

High-Capacity Backpack: Mammoth Carrier Tier A

The Mammoth Carrier is designed for long raids and large hauls, offering the maximum possible storage space. While it provides unparalleled capacity, it significantly reduces mobility, making you a slower target.
